Whole Lemon Blender Cake

Description

Lemon cake, what more do I have to say???? Tastes like summer!

Ingredients

  • 1 large skinned lemon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh squeezed lemon juice, plus zest for garnish (from 1 lemon)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • baking spray

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly coat the pan with baking spray and line with parchment. Spray the parchment paper with baking spray as well.
  2. Trim and discard lemon ends, slice the lemon into 8 wedges and remove seeds.
  3. Blend the lemon wedges, sugar, canola oil, sour cream, eggs and lemon extract.
  4. Incorporate the flour, baking powder and salt into the blender until smooth, thick batter is formed.
  5. Scrap sides to ensure everything is incorporated properly.
  6. Pour batter into pan and place in pre-heated oven for about 50 - 55 mins (or until toothpick comes out cleanly)
  7. Tent the cake with tin foil after about 30 mins in oven.
  8. Take cake out and let rest for about 10 mins. Remove cake and place on rack for another 20 mins.
  9. Whisk powdered sugar and lemon juice in a separate bowl until combined.
  10. Drizzle mixture over cake and garnish with lemon zest.
  11. ENOJOY!
