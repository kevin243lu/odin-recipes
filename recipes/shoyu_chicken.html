Shoyu Chicken

Description

Shoyu is the japanese word for soy sauce - the main character in this recipe. We let the chicken marinate in the soy sauce for an extravagent flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 whole onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespooon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced)

These are optional ingredients to enhance the flavor

  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika

Steps

  1. Wisk ALL ingredients EXCEPT chicken in a bowl.
  2. Add the chicken to the previously mixed ingredients and evenly coat.
  3. Plastic wrap the bowl and let marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  4. Preheat grill/pan to medium-high heat. Oil the surface
  5. Remove chicken from the marinate and place onto cooking surface.
  6. Cook until chicken is no longer pink or internal temperature is is at least 165 F (~15 mins on each side).
