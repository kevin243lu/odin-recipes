Honey Garlic Chicken Thighs

Description

This recipe is just as advertised. Honey, Garlic and Chicken thighs. Sweet chicken with tons of garlic and even more herbs. This light yet filling meal will be at the top of your recipe list when you are expecting company or just having a nice night in.

Ingredients

The ingredients you will need are:
  • 8 (5 ounce) Chicken thighs (boneless is preferred)
  • 1 cup honey
  • 7 cloves of garlic (chopped or to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch of onion powder
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup of diced onions
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of cilantro

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Season both sides of the chicken with salt and pepper
  3. Heat up the olive oil in a pan on medium-high heat
  4. Add the chicken to the pan and brown on each side (~3 to 5 min)
  5. Flip the chicken and add the onions and garlic
  6. Remove chicken and place on a plate when chicken is almost cooked
  7. Add honey, soy sauce and garlic powder to the pan then stir the contents to mix everything
  8. Return the chicken to the pan and slowly cook on medium heat - flip after 5 mins
  9. Check the internal temperature of the chicken (should be at least 165 F)
  10. Arrange chicken how you like on a plate and drizzle the sauce over the chicken
  11. ENJOY!
