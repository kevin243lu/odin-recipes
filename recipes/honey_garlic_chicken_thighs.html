Honey Garlic Chicken Thighs

Description

This recipe is just as advertised. Honey, Garlic and Chicken thighs. Sweet chicken with tons of garlic and even more herbs. This light yet filling meal will be at the top of your recipe list when you are expecting company or just having a nice night in.

Ingredients

The ingredients you will need are:
  • 8 (5 ounce) Chicken thighs (boneless is preferred)
  • 1 cup honey
  • 7 cloves of garlic (chopped or to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch of onion powder
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup of diced onions
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of cilantro
